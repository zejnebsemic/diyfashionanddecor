Get To Know Us


Welcome to Crafted With Love, where creativity meets innovation! Founded by two passionate DIY enthusiasts, Zejneb Semic and Vildana Delibasic, our platform is dedicated to bringing you unique ideas in fashion and decor. Guided by our mentor, Professor Amela Vatres, we aim to inspire a community that values creativity, sustainability, and self-expression. Dive into our world of DIY and discover how simple projects can add a touch of personal charm to your life.

Meet Our Team


Zejneb Semic


CEO & Founder


Zejneb is a first-year IT student at International Burch University, where she channels her love for technology and creativity into every project she undertakes. Zejneb is not only a student but also the CEO and Founder of Crafted With Love, a unique web platform centered around DIY fashion and decor. Her passion for DIY was sparked during her high school years when she began experimenting with transforming everyday items into fashionable and functional pieces. Crafted With Love is her way of sharing this passion with a broader audience, creating a space where others can find inspiration and practical guidance for their own DIY projects. With each tutorial, Zejneb aims to show how DIY can be a fun, accessible, and sustainable way to enhance personal style and home decor.

Vildana Delibasic


Art Director


Vildana , who shares Zejneb’s enthusiasm for DIY, serves as the Art Director for Crafted With Love. Also a first-year IT student at International Burch University, Vildana discovered her love for DIY as she explored creative ways to repurpose clothing and decor pieces.


Her background in art and eye for aesthetics perfectly complements Zejneb's technical skills. Together, they bring a unique blend of creativity and innovation to the platform. Vildana's role as Art Director allows her to infuse each project with visual charm, emphasizing the beauty and personality in handmade creations. Through Crafted With Love, she and Zejneb aim to inspire others to express themselves through personalized fashion and decor, believing that DIY projects can be both deeply personal and universally inspiring.

Professor Amela Vatres


Manager


Supporting this creative venture is Professor Amela Vatres, who serves as the Manager of Crafted With Love. With a background in business and education, Professor Vatres plays a key role in mentoring Zejneb and Vildana as they balance their academic responsibilities with their entrepreneurial aspirations. She offers strategic guidance, helping them set achievable goals and navigate the challenges of building a successful web platform. Professor Vatres’s expertise in project management and her understanding of the creative process make her an invaluable asset to the team.


By fostering an environment of growth and collaboration, she empowers Zejneb and Vildana to push the boundaries of their creativity while developing essential business skills. Her role extends beyond management—she is a mentor, advisor, and champion of their vision, guiding them as they turn their passion for DIY into a thriving community.
