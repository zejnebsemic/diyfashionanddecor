Get To Know Us

Welcome to Crafted With Love, where creativity meets innovation! Founded by a DIY enthusiast, Zejneb Semic, this platform is dedicated to providing you with unique fashion and home decor ideas. Led by my mentor, Professor Amela Vatres, we strive to inspire a community that values creativity, sustainability, and self-expression. Dive into the world of DIY and discover how simple projects can add some personal charm to your life.
