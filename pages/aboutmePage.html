Frequently Asked Questions

Crafted With Love (CWL) is a platform designed to inspire creativity and promote sustainability through DIY projects. Our mission is to help individuals personalize their homes, wardrobes, and lives by offering step-by-step tutorials for fashion, home decor, and personalized gifts. We believe that creativity has the power to transform everyday items into meaningful pieces that reflect your unique style and values.

The CWL community is open to everyone! Whether you are a complete beginner or a seasoned DIY enthusiast, our tutorials cater to all skill levels. Anyone who loves crafting, sustainability, and creative self-expression is welcome to explore, learn, and share their projects with our supportive community. Joining is free, and you can access our resources anytime to get inspired.

We love hearing from our creative audience! If you have a unique DIY idea, you can share it with us by filling out the Contact Form on our website. Alternatively, you can send an email with detailed information, photos, or videos of your project to zejneb.semic@stu.ibu.edu.ba. Selected projects may be featured on our platform, giving you the opportunity to inspire others while showcasing your work.
